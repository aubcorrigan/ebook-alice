Welcome to this online edition of Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland, the beloved classic written by Lewis Carroll. This whimsical tale follows young Alice as she falls down a rabbit hole into a fantastical world filled with curious creatures, riddles, and adventures.

This e-book contains the first three chapters of the story, each carefully formatted for comfortable reading on any device. You’ll find illustrations from the original edition, navigation links at the top and bottom of each page, and a design meant to enhance the magical atmosphere of the book.

Use the navigation links above or below to start reading. Whether you are revisiting this childhood favorite or discovering it for the first time, we hope you enjoy Alice’s journey into Wonderland.
